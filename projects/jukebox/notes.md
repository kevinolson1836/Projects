# NFC Jukebox

## Day 1

- found a really cool looking jukebox from minecraft on makerspace and had the idea to make it play real music 

## Day 2

- looked into hardware and landed on this esp32 based audio board (it sucks and didn't work.... dont recomnd that)
- ended up going with this hardware 
    - ESP32
    - NFC Tags Sticker with Original NTAG213 Chip NFC Stickers
    - 3.7V 4600mAH batterys
    - PN532 (NFC and RFID reader)
    - 2 28mm round 8 ohm 2 watt speakers
    - basic SD card
    - DFPlayer Mini
    - PLA filament in assorted colors
