# Minecraft Nether portal


## The idea

- I was looking around [Makerworld.com](https://makerworld.com/) for some new minecraft ideas and I stumbled on this Nether portal, I really liked the inserts idea and the LEDS so I wanted to recreate this for my self. I have a few ideas on how to do this diffrenttly 

-  [Minecraft Nether Portal LED Lamp (WLED Compatible)](https://makerworld.com/en/models/527032-minecraft-nether-portal-led-lamp-wled-compatible#profileId-444004)


## Day 1
- sketched up the design to fit a 1m LED strip just like the above link
- still a few things to sort out, I want to use USB-C to power it, I have some break out boards that I really like
- I need to find a nice filament color that looks like obsidian
